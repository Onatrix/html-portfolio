My top movies of all time

Spirited Away

Spirited Away is a 2001 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It tells the story of a young girl navigating a mysterious and magical world after her parents are transformed into pigs.

Inception

Inception is a 2010 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows a skilled thief who enters the dreams of others to steal secrets and faces challenges in a world where dreams can be manipulated.

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 drama film directed by Frank Darabont. It tells the story of a banker who is wrongfully imprisoned and forms a friendship with a fellow inmate, exploring themes of hope and resilience.
